Welcome to My College Foundation Day Diary!

Join us on a journey through a vibrant day filled with excitement and memorable experiences at our college. This website captures the essence of our campus life, featuring events like Bingo, the Intercollegiate Quiz Bowl Competition, and the dazzling La Paroza talent showcase. Explore various sections to relive the day through a timeline of events, photos, and videos that highlight the incredible moments shared among students.

We hope you enjoy this glimpse into our college day and the unforgettable memories we created together!

BINGO

As the excitement built before the Bingo event, we eagerly waited for the LED screen to be set up, anticipating the fun to come. When the game finally began, the atmosphere buzzed with hope and anticipation. Everyone around me was filled with enthusiasm, each of us hoping for that lucky win. Ultimately, one of our friends emerged victorious, which made the event even more special for us.

INTERCOLLEGIATE QUIZ BOWL COMPETITION

My heart was pounding as I prepared for the Quiz Bowl, knowing that I was one of the participants. With each round, my nerves calmed a bit as I found myself answering questions more confidently, supported by the cheers from my classmates. Although we didn't win, we were thrilled to be part of the competition, and it was a great learning experience for us.

LA PAROZA

Attending the La Paroza event was an electrifying experience. From dance performances to musical renditions, the stage came alive with diverse talents. Everyone in the audience was captivated by the energy and creativity on display.

CAMPUS ICONS

Our campus is full of iconic landmarks that hold a special place in every student’s heart. From the serene gardens to the bustling student union, these locations have become synonymous with our college experience. Take a look at some of the most beloved spots on campus!

As I reflect on our college’s foundation day, it’s hard not to smile at the incredible memories we created. From the nervous energy of the Quiz Bowl to the collective cheers of La Paroza, each event brought us closer together as a student body. I feel immensely grateful for the opportunity to participate and witness the talent and camaraderie that make our college community so unique. Until next year, these moments will be cherished!
